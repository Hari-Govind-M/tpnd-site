Why TPnD Matters

In today’s AI-driven, cloud-first world, you need more than just product access. You need strategic guidance and a dedicated advisor who can help you navigate complex technical presales, solution design, and rapid deployment. That’s where Technical Presales and Deployment (TPnD) shines.

TPnD is a global Microsoft organization that empowers partners with the resources, knowledge, and tools to plan, build, sell, and grow cutting-edge solutions. Whether it’s modernizing data centers, integrating AI and analytics, or enabling secure remote work, we partner with you every step of the way to accelerate your journey.

About Us

Our Mission

  • Empower Partners: We help you win deals, deliver impactful deployments, and stand out in a competitive market.
  • Accelerate Time-to-Market: We shorten project timelines with AI-driven best practices.
  • Enable Ongoing Innovation: We emphasize knowledge transfer, so you can continually innovate.

Our Partner Technical Consultants (PTCs) have deep expertise in Azure, Microsoft 365, Dynamics 365, the Power Platform, AI, and more. Think of us as your trusted advisors, guiding you through solution architecture, proofs of concept, deployment best practices, and post-deployment optimization.

Plan

The Plan phase focuses on discovery, assessment, and establishing a high-level roadmap. We examine your environment—on-premises, cloud, or hybrid—to identify technical gaps and AI opportunities. Together, we determine whether to rehost, refactor, or rearchitect workloads, aligning your technical path with business objectives.

Example: A mid-sized ISV wants to migrate a legacy .NET application to Azure while integrating AI. After evaluating on-prem VMs and databases, we outline a plan to containerize certain components, incorporate Azure Cognitive Services for new AI-driven features, and set up a phased deployment roadmap.

Build

In the Build phase, our PTCs collaborate with you on technical architecture & deployment. We ensure best practices around networking, security, identity, and data storage are followed, while guiding you through proofs of concept to validate performance and AI scenarios.

Example: You modernize a data platform with Azure Synapse Analytics, Data Lake, and Power BI for real-time insights. A quick proof-of-concept on historical data paves the way for advanced ML-based forecasting, significantly reducing time-to-market for your analytics solutions.

Sell

The Sell phase focuses on technical presales, helping you create effective demos, handle objections, and develop solid ROI/TCO arguments for customers. We can co-create scenario-based demonstrations—integrating AI, cloud-native services, or line-of-business apps—to show how Microsoft solutions address real-world customer challenges.

Example: A partner competes to deploy Microsoft 365 for a global manufacturing client. We build a custom Teams + Microsoft 365 Copilot demo that highlights collaboration, AI-assisted insights, and reduced IT complexity. This ensures the partner can confidently articulate the business value, winning the deal.

Grow

After success in the field, the Grow phase is about continuous improvement and expansion. We help you identify additional workloads—like IoT integration, machine learning pipelines, or co-sell opportunities—to further enhance your solution portfolio and deepen customer relationships.

Example: A retailer successfully migrates to Azure and wants to incorporate IoT sensors for predictive maintenance. We guide the integration of Azure Stream Analytics and Azure Machine Learning, delivering real-time anomaly detection. This new offering grows revenue streams while cementing the retailer’s trust in your services.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. Am I eligible for TPnD services?

If you’re part of the Microsoft AI Cloud Partner Program with a Solutions Partner, Legacy Gold/Silver, or Action Pack designation, you likely have TPnD benefits. Check Partner Center to confirm.

2. How long do TPnD engagements last?

It depends on your scenario. We aim for short, high-impact engagements—anywhere from a few days to a few weeks—to address key technical blockers or presales demos.

3. Is TPnD the same as Microsoft Support?

No. TPnD focuses on strategic enablement—e.g., architecture guidance and short-term deployment assistance—while Microsoft Support handles break-fix incidents.

4. Does TPnD offer custom code development?

We provide guidance on best practices, DevOps, and AI/ML integrations. We do not write or maintain proprietary code for you.

5. How do I submit a TPnD Services Request?

In Partner Center, go to Benefits > Technical Presales & Deployment and choose your advisory hours benefit. Fill out the form describing your scenario, and we’ll reach out within two business days.
