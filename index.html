Why TPnD Matters

In today’s AI-driven, cloud-first world, you need more than just product access. You need strategic guidance and a dedicated advisor who can help you navigate complex technical presales, solution design, and rapid deployment.

Technical Presales and Deployment (TPnD) is a global Microsoft organization that empowers partners with the resources, knowledge, and tools to plan, build, sell, and grow leading-edge solutions. Whether it’s modernizing data centers, integrating AI and analytics, or enabling secure remote work, we partner with you every step of the way to accelerate your journey.

Azure Infrastructure

Modernize legacy systems, migrate on-premises workloads to Azure, and leverage secure, scalable infrastructure for your customers.

Data & AI

Transform data into insights using services like Azure Synapse, Azure Machine Learning, or real-time analytics. Empower solutions with AI-driven capabilities and predictive models.

Digital & App Innovation

Develop cloud-native apps using microservices, containers, serverless architectures, and DevOps. Publish and monetize on the Microsoft commercial marketplace.

Modern Work

Deploy Microsoft 365, Teams, SharePoint, and Microsoft 365 Copilot to drive collaboration, productivity, and secure remote/hybrid work scenarios.

Business Apps

Elevate customer experiences with Dynamics 365 and the Power Platform (Power Apps, Power Automate, Power BI). Gain predictive insights and automated workflows.

Security

Protect identities, data, and endpoints with Microsoft Entra (Azure AD), Microsoft Defender, and best-in-class Zero Trust architectures. Integrate SIEM/SOAR with Microsoft Sentinel for advanced threat detection.

Plan, Build, Sell, Grow

Plan

During Plan, we assess your environment—on-prem, cloud, or hybrid—to identify strategic workloads and opportunities for AI or modernization. We map out a clear blueprint to guide your transformation.

Build

In Build, our Partner Technical Consultants collaborate on technical architecture, deployment best practices, and proofs of concept to validate performance and AI scenarios.

Sell

Sell focuses on technical presales support, scenario-based demos, and ROI/TCO analyses—ensuring you can articulate the value to clients and close deals effectively.

Grow

In Grow, we expand your reach—integrating new workloads like IoT or advanced AI, connecting you to co-sell opportunities, and optimizing existing deployments.
