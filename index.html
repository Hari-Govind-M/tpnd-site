Why TPnD Matters

In today’s AI-driven, cloud-first world, you need more than just product access. You need strategic guidance and a dedicated advisor to help you navigate complex technical presales, solution design, and rapid deployment. That’s where Technical Presales and Deployment (TPnD) comes in.

We are a global Microsoft organization that empowers partners with the resources, knowledge, and tools to plan, build, sell, and grow high-value solutions.

About Us

Our Mission

  • Empower Partners: Win deals, deliver impactful deployments, and stand out in a dynamic market.
  • Accelerate Time-to-Market: Shorten project timelines with AI-driven best practices.
  • Enable Ongoing Innovation: Focus on knowledge transfer so you can innovate continuously.

Our Partner Technical Consultants (PTCs) are experts in Azure, Microsoft 365, Dynamics 365, Power Platform, AI, and more. They guide you through architecture, proofs of concept, deployment best practices, and post-deployment optimizations.

Eligibility & Benefits

TPnD benefits are available to Microsoft partners in the Microsoft AI Cloud Partner Program, including Solutions Partner designations, Legacy Gold/Silver status, and Action Pack. If you’re an eligible partner, you can request Partner Advisory Hours in Partner Center for these services.

Check your advisory hours allotment in Partner Center, and if you see “Looks like you don’t have any benefits yet,” confirm you signed in with the correct account or check your membership status.
