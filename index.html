Why TPnD Matters

In today’s AI-driven, cloud-first world, simply having access to Microsoft products and platforms isn’t enough to stay competitive. You need strategic guidance, hands-on expertise, and a dedicated advisor to help you navigate complexities in presales, solution design, and rapid deployment. That’s where Technical Presales and Deployment (TPnD) comes in.

TPnD is a global Microsoft organization that empowers Microsoft Partners with the resources, knowledge, and tools to plan, build, sell, and grow high-value solutions. Whether you’re brand new to Azure, Microsoft 365, or Dynamics 365, or you’re looking to integrate advanced AI or IoT workloads, our Partner Technical Consultants (PTCs) are ready to support your journey from end to end.

The Role of Partner Technical Consultants (PTCs)

Our PTCs combine deep technical expertise with substantial real-world deployment experience. They’re your partners for:

  • Technical Presales
    Develop solution demos—highlighting AI, analytics, and cloud benefits—to resonate with customer needs and address common objections.
  • Solution Architecture
    Assess existing environments, propose target architectures, and guide you through proofs-of-concept for cloud migrations, AI enhancements, or app modernization.
  • Deployment Assistance
    Share best practices for initial setup, networking, security, and performance, ensuring your production rollout is stable, compliant, and scalable.
  • Post-Deployment Optimization
    Offer insights on performance tuning, security hardening, compliance, and next-generation innovations—such as advanced analytics, machine learning, or cognitive services.

Our Engagement Model: Plan – Build – Sell – Grow

We tailor each engagement according to your needs, following a four-phase approach:

1. Plan

Discovery & Assessment
Evaluate your current environment—on-prem, cloud, or hybrid—to identify technical gaps and AI opportunities.

High-Level Architecture
Determine the best approach (rehost, refactor, rearchitect, or greenfield) and align solution areas (infrastructure, data, AI) with your business goals.

Roadmap
Create a clear project roadmap so all stakeholders understand the project scope, timeline, and desired outcomes.

Example
A mid-sized ISV wants to migrate a legacy .NET app to Azure while integrating AI. After assessing on-prem VMs and databases, we outline a plan to rehost certain components and refactor others for containers, detailing how Azure Cognitive Services can enhance the user experience.

2. Build

Technical Architecture & Deployment
Our PTCs guide best practices around networking, security, identity, and data—ensuring a future-proof environment.

Proof-of-Concept & Pilot
Validate key workloads, AI prototypes, or performance metrics in a controlled environment before scaling up.

Knowledge Transfer
We coach your teams on resource management, DevOps processes, and monitoring so you’re self-sufficient after the engagement.

Example
You modernize a data analytics platform with Azure Synapse, Data Lake, and Power BI. A small proof-of-concept with historical sales data paves the way for full-scale AI-driven forecasting.

3. Sell

Technical Presales Demos
Showcase real-world scenarios—like AI-based image recognition—that directly address your customers’ business challenges.

Competitive Positioning & Objection Handling
Leverage our deep Microsoft expertise to counter concerns about solution complexity or cost.

ROI/TCO Analysis
Develop robust, data-driven business cases to illustrate the benefits of cloud transformation, AI adoption, or workload consolidation.

Example
A partner competes to deploy Microsoft 365 for a global manufacturing client. We help create a Teams + Microsoft 365 Copilot demo that emphasizes streamlined workflow, AI-assisted productivity, and reduced IT overhead.

4. Grow

Post-Deployment Optimization
Adopt continuous best practices for performance, scalability, governance, and compliance—crucial for expanding AI workloads.

Advanced Feature Adoption
Explore additional services (machine learning, data analytics, IoT) to drive ongoing innovation and revenue growth.

Co-Sell & Marketplace Alignment
If you’re a Microsoft partner, we can help align your solution for co-sell and commercial marketplace exposure.

Example
After migrating to Azure, a retailer wants to integrate IoT sensors for predictive maintenance. We help with Azure Stream Analytics and Azure Machine Learning for real-time anomaly detection, turning initial success into a broader advanced solution offering.

Understanding Eligibility & Benefits

How to Submit a TPnD Services Request

Sign in to Partner Center and ensure your organization is enrolled in the Microsoft AI Cloud Partner Program (Solutions Partner, Legacy Gold/Silver, Action Pack, etc.). Select Benefits, then choose Technical Presales & Deployment (TPnD). Pick your Advisory Hours benefit (if you have multiple, choose the right bucket), then create a case by providing a title, description, relevant product area (Azure, M365, Dynamics 365, etc.), and your full international dial code with preferred working hours. Once you submit, you’ll receive a confirmation page and email with your case number.

Important: If your account is only associated with a reseller/CSP program and not with the Microsoft AI Cloud Partner Program, you may see insufficient permissions. If you have no active membership offers, you’ll see “Looks like you don’t have any benefits yet.” Confirm you’re signed in with the correct account.
