Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: What if I need ongoing break‐fix or customization support?
A: TPD focuses on short-term engagements for presales and deployment guidance, not ongoing break‐fix. Consider Premier Support, Advanced Support for Partners, or other offerings for continuous support.

Q2: Can TPD help with AI-driven chatbots for customer service scenarios?
A: Absolutely. TPD can offer best practices integrating Microsoft Copilot for Service, AI Builder, or the Microsoft Digital Contact Center Platform to enhance your customer service capabilities.

Q3: Are there limitations to the number of TPD engagements I can request?
A: It depends on your partner status. Solutions Partners and Legacy Gold/Silver often have unlimited presales support, while Action Pack or Partner Success Core may use advisory hours.

Q4: Do we have to use all Microsoft technologies, or can we integrate with third‐party platforms?
A: You can integrate third‐party solutions. TPD consultants will advise on best practices for bridging Microsoft and non‐Microsoft services seamlessly.

Q5: How do we get started with publishing our vertical solution on the Microsoft commercial marketplace?
A: TPD can guide you through certification, go‐to‐market strategy, co‐sell alignment, and listing best practices, ensuring global visibility for your solution.
