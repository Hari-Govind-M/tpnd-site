Build

In the Build phase, our Partner Technical Consultants guide you through designing and deploying your solution. This includes:

  • Technical Architecture & Deployment: Best practices for networking, security, identity, and data.
  • Proof-of-Concept & Pilot: Validate AI prototypes, performance metrics, and user adoption.
  • Knowledge Transfer: Your teams receive step-by-step coaching on resource management, DevOps, and monitoring.

Example Scenario: You modernize a data analytics platform with Azure Synapse Analytics, Data Lake, and Power BI. A small proof-of-concept on historical sales data sets the stage for full-scale AI-driven forecasting.
