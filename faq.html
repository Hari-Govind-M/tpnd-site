Frequently Asked Questions

Am I eligible for TPnD services?

If you’re part of the Microsoft AI Cloud Partner Program with a Solutions Partner, Legacy Gold/Silver, or Action Pack designation, you likely have TPnD benefits. Check Partner Center for details.

How long do TPnD engagements last?

It depends on project scope. We aim for short, high-impact interventions (a few days to a few weeks) to resolve key blockers and drive momentum.

Is TPnD the same as Microsoft Support?

No. TPnD focuses on strategic enablement like solution architecture and short-term deployment assistance. Microsoft Support handles break-fix incidents.

Does TPnD offer custom code development?

We advise on best practices, AI/ML integrations, and DevOps. However, we do not write or maintain proprietary code for partners.

Do you provide official training or certifications?

TPnD engagements include knowledge transfer, but for formal training or certifications, please explore Microsoft Learn.

How do I submit a TPnD Services Request?

Sign in to Partner Center > Benefits > Technical Presales & Deployment. Choose your advisory hours benefit, fill the form, and provide a detailed scenario. You’ll get a confirmation with a case number. A Partner Technical Consultant will reach out within two business days.
