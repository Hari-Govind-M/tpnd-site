A Day in the Life of a Partner Technical Consultant (PTC)

Being a Partner Technical Consultant (PTC) for Azure Infrastructure is like being at the center of a dynamic, ever-changing ecosystem. My day is a blend of technical problem-solving, strategic enablement, and moments of joy—both professional and personal. Here’s a glimpse into what my day looks like:

Morning Routine: The Boards Over Emails

The first thing I do after logging in isn’t checking emails but diving into our operational boards. These boards are my compass, showing my OPEX metrics and giving me a glimpse of the day ahead. They highlight the priorities, pending cases, and where my focus needs to be. Emails come next, serving as reminders of follow-ups and partner commitments.

Kicking Off Consultations

On a good day, I get to engage in three partner consultations. It’s not just about volume but the quality of these interactions that makes the work rewarding.

First Task: I start by setting up a call with a partner who wants guidance on moving NetApp Files from one tenant to another. With 30 minutes to spare before the call, I shoot off an email and prep myself for the discussion.

Balancing Multiple Demands

While I’m getting ready for the NetApp call, I remember a request from my colleague in Modern Work. A partner is exploring advanced AI solutions, having already mastered CoPilot, and needs support for Azure AI. I schedule an hour-long session for later, ensuring a buffer for unexpected overruns.

Tackling the Day’s First Challenge

The first case of the day lands in my queue: a complex SAP HANA database migration from on-premises to Azure Disaster Recovery (DR). This requires collaboration with my Data and AI colleague, as no challenge is tackled in isolation. Before diving into the details, I hop on the NetApp call.

Turning Conversations into Opportunities

The NetApp session goes smoothly. Not only do we address the migration challenge, but the discussion pivots to Sentinel—a topic that excites the partner. Sensing an opportunity, I propose an enablement session to deepen their understanding of security. I immediately connect with my go-to PTC for security to plan this.

Licensing… a Curveball

Out of the blue, a licensing case comes up. While it’s not in my scope, I know the partner needs help. I set up a call to guide them toward the right resources, ensuring no roadblocks in their journey.

The Magic of Collaboration

The day continues with a call addressing Azure AI and Modern Work needs. The partner’s progress in mastering these solutions feels like a shared win. Post-call, I take a brief pause to play with my daughter—a joyful moment that rejuvenates me before diving back into work.

Deep Dives and Research

With two hours before my next meeting, I delve into the SAP HANA DR case. The partner’s detailed documentation saves me time and assumptions, allowing me to focus on meaningful research. Challenges like these emphasize why clarity in requirements is key.

Community Engagement and Learning

After lunch and a few CoPilot-assisted tasks, I join a community call. These sessions are treasure troves of insights, equipping me with fresh ideas to help partners build, sell, and grow.

The Big Collaboration

A financial and government entity case hits my desk next. With six solution areas involved, it’s an all-hands-on-deck scenario. I initiate discussions with colleagues, knowing CAF Landing Zones and Azure Stack HCI will be pivotal.

Closing the Loop

As the day winds down, my security PTC sends a detailed Sentinel report, asking for a follow-up call. The gaps identified are critical, and we schedule a session for tomorrow. In the meantime, I wrap up learnings, feedback sessions, and a team meeting.

Reflection and Growth

Nine hours later, I reflect on the magic of the day. Each interaction, challenge, and learning moment contributes to making my partners, colleagues, and myself better than before. Whether it’s the light of successful consultations or the dark magic of complex problems, every day is an adventure I wouldn’t trade for anything.

The PTC Way

Being a PTC is not just a job; it’s a journey of continuous growth, collaboration, and impact. And at the heart of it all, the belief that every problem has a solution or a workaround drives us to achieve more.
