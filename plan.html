Plan

The Plan phase focuses on discovery, assessment, and establishing a high-level roadmap. We evaluate your current environment—whether on-premises, cloud, or hybrid—and identify technical gaps and AI opportunities.

  1. Discovery & Assessment: We identify challenges, key workloads, and AI opportunities.
  2. High-Level Architecture: We help you decide on approaches like rehost, refactor, or greenfield.
  3. Roadmap: We create a plan that aligns your technical investments with strategic business goals.

Example Scenario: A mid-sized ISV wants to migrate a legacy .NET application to Azure while adding AI features. After assessing on-prem VMs and databases, we outline a plan to rehost certain components, refactor others for containers, and integrate Azure Cognitive Services.
